PHP Twig bundle Cheat-sheet

Andrew Fricke made a PHP Twig bundle for Sublime Text and Texmate and here is a snippets cheat-sheet for it.

For each snippet you've got the string in the purple block and the result below.

For those who are not familiar with the snippets syntax, the ${x:string} are mark positions to cycle through by pressing TAB and the $0 is the exit mark.
